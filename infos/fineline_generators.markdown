# fineline Generators

Here is an summary of our generators:


	
	$ rails g strangecms:install
generates the CMS

	
	$ rails g strangecms:update
updates the CMS

	
	$ rails g strangecms:modul *name*
generates a new modul-gem

	
	$ rails g strangecms:theme *name*
generates a new theme-gem
